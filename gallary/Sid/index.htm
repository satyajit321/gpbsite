SIDU, MY GRANDSON:
As my date of retirment from service approached I was blessed with the news of birth of our grand son on 20th. March, 2000. He was named Siddhartha alias Sidu. I demitted my service on first July and rushed to Minnesota to see him.It gave us great pleasure to welcome him to our family.We have seen him growing up from a serene baby to a naughty, yet vibrant boy of today(2008). He is in class three. To his friend at school he is known as "Sid". He now swims, paints and learn Karate also. Here are a few of our favourite pictures of him that we would like to share with our friends and fans as thoughts of him now is our favourite pastime nay a full time occupation.
